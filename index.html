Ryan Reynolds

is a Postdoctoral Scholar in the Finlayson-Pitts Research Group at the University of California, Irvine. He earned his PhD in Physical Chemistry in 2024 from UC Berkeley, where he was a member of the Wilson Research Group at Lawrence Berkeley National Lab. His research focuses on the chemistry of organic molecules in aerosol particles, specifically on the effects which temperature and phase have on their chemical transformations.
